Why Companies Migrate Away from Datorama (MCI)

Many businesses move off Datorama to reduce unpredictable costs, gain control of their data models, and unlock faster analytics at scale.

Rising Costs

Costs often scale by rows processed or events ingested — leading to unpredictable bills as volume grows.

Platform Limitations

Some teams face slow visualizations, limited control over data models, and constrained transformation flexibility.

Open Architecture & Scale

Cloud warehouses and SQL-based stacks (Redshift, BigQuery, Snowflake) provide predictable costs and scale for high-volume analytics.
